What We Do?

We leverage state-of-the-art Siamese Neural Network technology to achieve impressive signature accuracy rates.

UPLOAD DATA

This initial step involves inputting the relevant signature data into the system. This can include scanned images or digital files of signatures to be verified.

TEST

Once the data is uploaded, the system initiates the verification process. It employs advanced algorithms to analyze the provided signature against reference samples.

GET RESULT

This step delivers a percentage-based evaluation, indicating the degree of similarity between the provided signature and the reference data, ensuring a concise outcome.
